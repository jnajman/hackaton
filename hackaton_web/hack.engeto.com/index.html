People often ask

  • I have no IT background.

    You have to have at least some knowledge. Please check our training course, engeto.cz/python-free

  • Is hackathon in Czech or English.

    Primary language for hackathon is Czech Language.

  • Can I sleep at Impact Hub?

    Yes, of course, just let us know. We would like to know how many people are planning to sleep here. Number of places available is limited, please let us know in advance.

  • Can I win some prizes?

    Yes, of course, check what our partners have donated. We will not reward only those with the best and most advanced code, but also those who will be very active and eager to know something new.
